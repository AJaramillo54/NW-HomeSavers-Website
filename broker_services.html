    Services for Brokers

    Short Sale Negotiations

    As you know, short sales can take a considerable amount of your time and attention. Allow us to handle your short sale negotiations from the beginning of the process until the end. Special Debt Management Service Provider licensing allows us to negotiate on your behalf. Simply list and market a property and we handle the rest of the process for you. With or without an offer on the home in question, we can work with your client’s mortgage company to get any short sale negotiation initiated.

    Get Started

    Getting a short sale started with us is fast and simple. Just follow the steps below:

    1. Request a starter packet from us.
    2. Complete all of the documents found within your starter packet.
    3. Submit your completed starter packet by fax, email, or post at your convenience.

    By completing and submitting the documents found within your starter packet, you will allow us to review your file and initiate your short sale with the appropriate mortgage company. Your starter packet also includes a handful of forms for the seller to complete, as well as a checklist of other documents the bank will need to move your short sale forward. We will also request necessary items from the appropriate listing agent and title company in order to initiate the short sale negotiation process.
