    Services for Homeowners

    Short Sale Negotations

    A short sale is a real estate transaction in which your mortgage lender agrees to take less than what you owe on your unpaid balance. A short sale on your home can be beneficial, as both you and your lender would avoid foreclosure and the negative impact that one would have on your credit score. By avoiding a foreclosure, you can start rebuilding your credit faster, and may be able to purchase another home sooner. If your lender agrees to a short sale, you can sell your home and payoff up to 100% of your mortgage balance with the proceeds.

    We at NW HomeSavers have negotiated thousands of short sales, helping homeowners sell their homes and build debt-free financial foundations. We make no profits until your short sale is complete, and our services come at no cost to you or the buyer of your home.
