    • About the owner.

    Chris Garfield

    Chris Garfield, the owner of NW HomeSavers, has experience with all aspects of short sales and has been negotiating them since 2005. Chris managed the short sale department of a large bank when the short sale market was in its early stages of development, an experience that provided him with the skills to establish NW HomeSavers from the ground up. He now dedicates much of his time to train his employees and to build a solid team of short sale negotiators.

    About your Debt Management Service Provider.

    NW HomeSavers

    Located in the beautiful state of Oregon, we are a Debt Management Service Provider, licensed to negotiate short sales as a service to real estate agents and their clients. Our team consists of highly skilled individuals that come from a variety professional backgrounds. Together we have over 30 years of experience in banking, real estate, short sale negotiations. We understand the complexities of the short sale process and work hard to keep each case as simple as possible for all parties involved. We go the extra mile to gain full resolution of all mortgages, liens and judgments found on our clients' property title reports.
